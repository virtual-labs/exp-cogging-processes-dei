### Procedure

1. Go to the **Simulation** tab.
2. Select the experiment you want to perform.
3. Read the **theory** of the selected experiment carefully.
4. Observe the **video simulation** attentively to understand the process.
5. Once finished, proceed to the **next step** of the simulation.
6. After completing all the steps of the experiment, click on the **“Back to Selection”** button.
7. Select the **next experiment** you want to perform and **repeat the above steps** from Step 1 onward.
