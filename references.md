### References

1. Groover, M. P. (2020). *Fundamentals of Modern Manufacturing: Materials, Processes, and Systems*. Wiley.  
   — Explains hot forging processes including cogging, billet formation, and grain refinement.

2. Kalpakjian, S., & Schmid, S. R. (2014). *Manufacturing Processes for Engineering Materials*. Pearson Education.  
   — Covers ingot-to-billet processing, cogging principles, and microstructural improvements.

3. ASM International. (2005). *ASM Handbook, Volume 14: Forming and Forging*. ASM International.  
   — Detailed reference on cogging, hot forging, grain flow, and defect elimination in cast metals.

4. Lange, K. (1985). *Handbook of Metal Forming*. McGraw-Hill.  
   — Provides theoretical and practical insights into billet cogging, die arrangements, and deformation behavior.

5. Altan, T., Ngaile, G., & Shen, G. (2005). *Cold and Hot Forging: Fundamentals and Applications*. ASM International.  
   — Discusses industrial cogging operations, strain generation, and mechanical property enhancement.
